The biggest tournaments and leagues

A selection of the largest sports tournaments in the world from various games

  • LEC or League of Legends European Championship is the European professional sports league of Riot Games focused on League of Legends. It is one of the four largest LoL leagues of the world and is represented by the ten best home teams. League was founded in 2013 in Germany under the original name European League of Legends Championship Series (EU LCS). From this name later dropped and the league was renamed the LEC. The league has a task select the three best teams in the European region to be Europe to represent at the World Championships in League of Legends, the largest sports events of the year for the League of Legends scene. The whole process The league is broadcast live via the Twitch platform, YouTube, or progaming television channels.

  • ESL Pro league is Counter-Strike: Global Offensive professional sports league. It is based on four regions: Europe, America, Asia and Oceania and currently includes each season 24 teams, of which 12 permanent teams of partners.

  • Six invitational is an annual professional tournament Rainbow Six Siege hosted by Ubisoft developer and publisher. The tournament, located in Montreal, home of the Ubisoft studio working on this game, gathers the best teams from around the world. The event ends at the end of the previous year and operates as an informal global one cup.

  • Fortnite World Cup is an annual competition in sports based on the video game Fortnite, with an introductory final events will take place July 26 - 28, 2019 at Arthur Stadium Ashe Stadium in Flushing, New York. In various competitions he was k available a total prize pool of $ 30 million.

  • International, often abbreviated TI, is annual tournament organized by Valve. In 2015, Valve increased the number of hosted events and established the Dota Major Championships, which integrates The International into its structure. International remains the largest and most prestigious event on the Major circuit Championship. The first international took place in Cologne, Germany during Gamescom to introduce Dota 2 worldwide audience. It attracted attention thanks to a stunning prize pool of 1.6 million USD (with $ 1 million for the champion). 16 teams were invited to played for the title. In 2012, International moved to Seattle in Washington. The pricing fund remained at $ 1.6 million USD. It has been invited only 14 teams; the other two slots were awarded to regional winners qualifications. From 2013, the Dota 2 community can contribute to the prize pool. The total price of the International Year 2013 increased to 2.8 million USD. The prize fund exceeded $ 10 million in 2014 and in 2016 $ 20 million. In 2018, after six years in Seattle, International held in Vancouver, Canada. In 2019, The International held in Shanghai, China, the prize pool exceeded 34 million $, making it the largest prize pool for any single tournament in sports until The International 2020 was surpassed.
