Esport in CZ

History and information of Esport in the Czech Republic. I also added some Czech teams etc.

The history of competitive computer gaming is connected with development telecommunications technologies, mainly computers, by dissemination internet connection and the development of computer games. Other basics laid the development of organizations associating computer players. The first ever such an association in the Czech Republic was the team The NecroRaisers, whose origin dates back to 1.1.1997. Playing computer games for a professional level is among the activities that are not physically demanding and do not perform thus the definition of sport. Competitive competitions have clear rules and competitive character.

Until 2010, Českomoravský existed in electronic sports progaming association. His task was to increase the level of professionalism playing in the Czech Republic. One of the main goals of this association was also organizing the culmination of the whole year in the form of the Czech Championship Republic in computer games, which has been held in Brno since 2002 INVEX exhibition, later known as DIGITEX. Between 2008 and 2011, the Czech Championships did not arrange. However, since 2011 it has been held again, this time in Seč. AND when his own union ceased to function, he took over most of its functions community portal PLAYzone.cz. In 2013 and 2014, the Czech Republic received more a bigger stamp when it was moved from Jezerka to the Prague Exhibition Grounds in Letňany as a part of our largest gaming fair FOR GAMES. From In 2015, the final tournament of the Czech Computer Games Championship will take place on The Brno Exhibition Center BVV as part of the IN-JOY trade fair, which is attracting more and more people masses of fans of professional and semi-professional computer gaming her.
