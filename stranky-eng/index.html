What is Esport?

It is an organized competitive play of players or teams, in any game, on any platform (PC, console, mobile). The competitions are clear rules and can be attended by both amateurs and professionals players.

Among the most popular sports games are probably CS: GO, League of Legends or maybe Dota 2.
